Maple Story Introduction
[New Job Adele]
Adel, a knight who was stuck in Subspace for a long time After a long time, a boy's earnest prayer is summoned to the city of art, Lithuania.
-------------------------------------------------------------------------
Adele is a high reef, a warrior who uses magic to create ether swords.
Not only to blow ether swords to pierce or rush enemies, Willing Aether Swords can also chase enemies on their own.
After the 5th job, try colorful and powerful skills.
[Adele Support] During the support period, we also offer a variety of items that suit Adel's dignity.
■ Event Period: January 16 (Thursday), 20:20-11:59 PM
